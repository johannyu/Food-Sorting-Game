This is not a game, it's real life. Eat as much good food as you can before you die to earn coins. Careful not to eat the unhealthy foods, your lifespan will shorten; you'll lose energy as well.
You also lose more energy as you age, so don't skip meals!


There are 3 categories of food: healthy, neutral, and unhealthy.

Healthy: Earns 2 coins and increases energy significantly.
Neutral: Earns 1 coin and Increases energy slightly.
Unhealthy: Minus 2 years of your total lifespan and lose energy.
